This document maps the tables and figures presented in the manuscript to the exact output files and columns generated by the software workflow.

## 1. Manuscript Tables

### Table 1: Single-Seed Sweep Validation Summary ($d=6$)
*   **Source File:** [single_seed_sweep.csv](file:///Users/fatah/Documents/d6mubOptimization-v2/results/summaries/single_seed_sweep.csv)
*   **Mapping Description:** Reports the final best loss, execution time, and number of near-exact basis pairs for a single fixed validation seed across $n = 2, \dots, 7$ bases.
*   **Key Columns:** `n_bases`, `best_loss`, `time_taken`, `near_exact_pairs`

### Table 2: Precision Sensitivity Campaign Summary ($d=6$)
*   **Source File:** [precision_comparison_summary.csv](file:///Users/fatah/Documents/d6mubOptimization-v2/results/summaries/precision_comparison_summary.csv)
*   **Mapping Description:** Compares optimization outcomes between `complex128` (double precision) and `complex64` (single precision) over 100 random seeds for $n \in \{3, 4, 5, 6\}$.
*   **Key Columns:** 
    *   `dtype`: Complex precision identifier.
    *   `loss_min`, `loss_median`, `loss_max`: Minimum, median, and maximum total loss across the 100 runs.
    *   `near_exact_min`, `near_exact_median`, `near_exact_max`: Counts of pairs with maximum deviation $\le 10^{-6}$.

### Table 3: Hardware Optimization Execution Speed (CPU vs. GPU)
*   **Source File:** [hardware_benchmark_summary.csv](file:///Users/fatah/Documents/d6mubOptimization-v2/results/summaries/hardware_benchmark_summary.csv)
*   **Mapping Description:** Reports the execution times in seconds for 1000 optimization steps across dimensions $d \in \{6, 12, 24, 48, 96\}$ comparing standard CPU execution to GPU execution.
*   **Key Columns:** `dimension`, `cpu_complex128_seconds`, `gpu_complex128_seconds`, `cpu_complex64_seconds`, `gpu_complex64_seconds`
